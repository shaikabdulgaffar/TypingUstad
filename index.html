About Typing Ustad

Master Your Typing Skills

Typing Ustad is a modern, feature-rich typing practice application designed to help you improve your typing speed and accuracy through engaging exercises and tests.

Speed Tests

Accurate WPM measurement with detailed statistics and progress tracking.

Practice Mode

Unlimited practice sessions with no time pressure for skill building.

Visual Keyboard

Interactive keyboard highlighting to learn proper finger placement.

Progress Tracking

Comprehensive statistics to monitor your improvement over time.

Customizable

Dark/light themes, font sizes, and various display options.

Responsive

Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
